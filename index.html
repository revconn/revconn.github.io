ABOUT US

Built by people who've actually stood on the site.

What we're trying to do

We build software to eliminate unnecessary work, not create more of it. Every product we ship is judged by one standard: does it remove a task from someone's day, or add to it? That discipline is what separates genuine automation from another dashboard to check.

Why choose us

Because we're lazy — and we mean that as a compliment to our users. We believe the best software removes work rather than adding another dashboard to check. Automation should mean fewer clicks, not more.

"We build for people who'd rather spend their time on the work that matters — not the paperwork around it."

Who we are

We're NICMAR alumni who came up through the construction industry before we came up through code. Our team has felt the pain of tracking a project on paper, and builds the tools we wish we'd had on site.
